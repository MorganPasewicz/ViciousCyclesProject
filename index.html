We live by the scout's motto- Leave no trace. We respect our environment and live to keep our world untouched while still experiencing the beauty of nature. We are carbon neutral and source our bikes and products from sustainable sources. After cycling for years, I wanted to have a place to shop that reflects the values that I live by- leave no trace, keep our environment clean, and live free.

Our customers want to live a clean and healthy lifestyle. They want to enjoy cycling and spending time outdoors guilt-free. We sell Moutain bikes and accessories that will keep our customers up with the mountain biking lifestyle.
